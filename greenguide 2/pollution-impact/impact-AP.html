Air Pollution

Air pollution not only contaminates our atmosphere but also affect human health and ecosystem. Industrial smog released by coal burning, contributes to human respiratory diseases such as breathing problems.

---Air pollution irritates our respiratory systems causing us to sneeze and cough. Air pollution also increases the probability of heart attacks due to inhalation of soot particles.

---Air pollution highly associated with acid deposition, which damages building, metals, and harms forest. Acid deposition causes leaching of toxic metals in the soil which indirectly kills our trees indirectly by changing the pH of the soil. In this way, acid deposition also strongly affects aquatic ecosystem, and agriculture plants.
