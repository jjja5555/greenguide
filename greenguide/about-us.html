About Us

In 2015, Green Guide Inc. is established in Seattle, US. In 2016, with the help of Microsoft, China and Guiyang government of Guizhou province in Southwest China, Green Guide Inc. established a branch company in Guiyang, China. In 2017 January, www.lovegreenguide.com website is opened for public to use.

Green Guide Inc. is devoted to apply information technology to realize green supply chain. It helps the public to participate, share and integrate environmental information. Hope it can be a sucessful solution for China’s environmental issues, and rebuild a good relationship between human and environment.
